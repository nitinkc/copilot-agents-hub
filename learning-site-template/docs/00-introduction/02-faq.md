## Learning Strategy

??? question "How should I read this guide?"
    Start with [Getting Started](../GETTING_STARTED.md), then follow the [Learning Path](01-learning-path.md). The guide progresses from fundamentals to advanced.

??? question "Can I skip sections?"
    Yes, if you already understand fundamentals. But we recommend reading the [Quick Reference](../reference/01-quick-reference.md) first to align on terminology.

??? question "How long does this take?"
    - **Fundamentals only:** 2–3 hours
    - **Through Intermediate:** 4–5 hours
    - **Full guide:** 6–8 hours
    
    Read at your own pace — no time pressure.

??? question "I'm stuck on a concept. What should I do?"
    1. Re-read the article slowly
    2. Check the [Quick Reference](../reference/01-quick-reference.md) for definitions
    3. Look for similar concepts in related articles
    4. Try explaining it to someone else
    5. Work through examples step-by-step

??? question "Is there a better way to learn this besides reading?"
    This guide focuses on **text + diagrams + examples**. Combined with hands-on practice in real projects, it's the most effective approach.
